Auto Accidents

"In 2018, the last year before the COVID pandemic, there were over 140,000 auto accidents in Wisconsin alone."

Being injured in a car accident can be a terrifying experience. Calling one of the attorneys that WIN recommends will relieve the stress and pressure from the legal case and let you focus on getting better as quickly as possible.
Any time there is an injury with a legal case, proper and thorough documentation are immeasurably important. Additionally, it's important for you to begin treatment as soon as possible so the insurance company will not be able to argue you got hurt some other way. By choosing WIN, we will work to get you an appointment within the same week you contact us, if not the same day.
