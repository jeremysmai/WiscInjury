About Us

Wisconsin Injury Network is a single contact organization to get you the care you need after an accident. Call us and you do not have to find your own way through the often confusing process of getting help. Our network consists of professionals that are experts with the medical and legal procedures that follow an injury. By calling us, you can rest assured that the providers reaching out to you are experienced and qualified so you can focus on healing and getting back to doing what you love.

We are primarily focused in the Milwaukee area, however, we are always expanding to cover more of Wisconsin.
